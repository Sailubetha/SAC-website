Sankranthi Sambaralu
A Grand Celebration of Tradition & Joy!

Vignan’s Lara Institute of Technology & Science was filled with festive cheer during Sankranthi Sambaralu, uniting students, staff, and faculty in celebration. The campus buzzed with energy, cultural pride, and joy as various traditional events brought everyone together.

📍 Where: Vignan’s Lara Institute of Technology & Science
📅 When: 6th October, 2024

🎨 A Colorful Start

  • Rangoli : The SAC team kickstarted the celebration with a Rangoli competition, where students showcased their creativity with stunning designs, setting a vibrant tone for the day.
  • Mehendi – Embracing Tradition: The Mehendi corner drew enthusiastic participants as students adorned their hands with intricate and elegant henna designs.
  • Cultural Extravaganza: The festive mood was further lifted by soulful singing and lively dance performances, creating a joyful and energetic ambiance.
  • Kite Flying – Soaring High with Festive Spirit: Students then engaged in a thrilling kite-flying competition, filling the sky with colorful kites and celebrating the essence of Sankranthi.

🔥 Bhogi Fire – A Symbol of Renewal

As the evening approached, the Bhogi fire was ceremoniously lit by our Principal, HODs, and Deans, marking a significant moment in the celebration. This was followed by a traditional dance around the fire, performed by girl faculty and students, symbolizing the burning away of the past and welcoming new beginnings.

💫 Pongal Cooking – A Taste of Tradition

Amidst the festivities, students engaged in Pongal cooking, filling the air with the delightful aroma of this traditional dish, embodying the spirit of togetherness and gratitude.

✨ A Celebration to Remember!

Sankranthi Sambaralu at Vignan’s Lara was a perfect blend of tradition, joy, and community spirit. The event honored cultural heritage while fostering togetherness, leaving behind beautiful memories for everyone.

📸 Glimpses of the Celebration

Here’s to more celebrations, shared happiness, and the essence of Sankranthi!
