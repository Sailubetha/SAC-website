Honoring Strength & Spirit:
A Joyous Women’s Day Celebration at Vignan’s Lara!

Vignan’s Lara proudly celebrated International Women’s Day with enthusiasm, honoring the remarkable contributions, resilience, and achievements of our lady faculty and girl students. The event was a perfect blend of fun, appreciation, and inspiration, making it a truly memorable occasion!

💃 A Celebration of Joy & Togetherness

The day was packed with exciting games, heartfelt speeches, and moments of appreciation. Our participants enthusiastically engaged in interactive activities that brought out their creativity, teamwork, and laughter:

  • 🎶 Guess the Song – A fun challenge where participants identified popular tunes.
  • 🎤 Sing the Lyrics – A lively sing-along session that revealed hidden musical talents.
  • 🛒 Guess the Grocery – A unique twist where participants identified grocery items through creative clues.
  • 🎬 Guess the Movie – A cinematic challenge guessing films based on hero, heroine, and director clues.

💡 Inspiring Words & Appreciation

The celebration culminated in an insightful and empowering address by our Principal, Dr. K. Phaneendra Kumar sir, who highlighted the significance of Women’s Day and the indispensable role women play in our society and institution. This was followed by inspiring messages from Heads of Departments, Deans, and SAC Convenors, who emphasized the importance of supporting and uplifting women in all fields.

🎁 Tokens of Gratitude

As a heartfelt gesture, gifts were presented to all lady faculty members and the dedicated girl SAC students, who played a vital role in making this celebration a grand success.

🌟 A Day to Remember!

The Women’s Day celebration at Vignan’s Lara was more than an event—it was a tribute to the strength, resilience, and achievements of women. With laughter, camaraderie, and a shared spirit of empowerment, the day was a testament to the college’s commitment to creating an inclusive and supportive environment.

Empowered women empower the world! Let’s continue celebrating, uplifting, and supporting each other—today and always!

Here's to strong women and stronger communities. 💐 Happy Women's Day from Vignan’s Lara!
